### 🧠 About Me

I'm a **Software Engineer** and **AI Systems specialist** focused on building production-grade **LLM pipelines, RAG architectures, and autonomous agent systems**.

As **Founder & CTO** at **[ThreePoints Technology](https://threepointstechnology.com.br/)**, I bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and real-world enterprise automation — combining **LLM fine-tuning, dataset engineering, and SecDevOps** to ship systems that are intelligent, secure, and scalable.
